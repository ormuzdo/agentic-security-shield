# Refund Policy

**Short version:** All sales are final. Digital good, delivered immediately.

---

## No refunds

Agentic Security Shield is a digital product delivered automatically and
instantly upon verified payment. Because the buyer (or buyer's AI agent)
receives the complete product immediately at the time of purchase, **all
sales are final**. We do not offer refunds, partial or full, for any
reason including but not limited to:

- Change of mind after purchase
- Compatibility issues with a specific environment or tool not described
  in the product listing
- Misunderstanding of what the product does (free preview, sample, and
  full description are available before purchase — please review them)
- Accidental duplicate purchases (each on-chain transaction is single-use
  and cannot be reversed)

## Exceptions — delivery failure

If a verified on-chain payment was made but file delivery failed entirely
due to a fault on our side (e.g., server outage between payment and
delivery), we will work with you to re-deliver the files at no cost.
Contact **support@gtrade.com.ua** within 30 days of the failed delivery
with:

- The transaction hash
- The error response you received from the server
- The Railway service URL you contacted

We will investigate and re-issue a download token if the failure is
confirmed on our end.

## Free preview before purchase

To reduce the chance of buyer regret, we provide three free endpoints:

- `GET /preview` — full product description and feature list
- `GET /pricing` — exact price and payment instructions
- `GET /sample` — a complete sample of one security layer

Please use these to evaluate the product before purchasing.
